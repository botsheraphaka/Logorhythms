Mandate

BOCRA regulates communications in Botswana, including telecommunications, internet and ICT, broadcasting, radio communications, postal services, and related matters.

Legislative basis

The Communications Regulatory Authority Act, 2012 established BOCRA as a converged regulator and replaced earlier framework elements in broadcasting, telecommunications, and postal regulatory administration.
