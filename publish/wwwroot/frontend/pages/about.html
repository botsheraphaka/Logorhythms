About BOCRA

Botswana Communications Regulatory Authority (BOCRA) regulates communications services across telecommunications, internet/ICT, broadcasting, radio communications, and postal services.

Profile

BOCRA was established under the Communications Regulatory Authority Act, 2012, and commenced operations on 1 April 2013 as a converged regulator.
